Kerala Solar Policy

State Policy Highlights

  • Maximum sanctioned load: 99%
  • Maximum load: 2 MW
  • Electricity generated by rooftop solar system shall not be more than 90% of the electricity consumption at the end of the settlement period.
  • Cumulative capacity of local distribution transformer: MAXIMUM 30%
  • Net Metering: ALLOWED
  • Solar rooftop mandatory for new residential buildings with plot size more than 500 sq. yards and for commercial offices, malls, complexes (3% to 5% of sanctioned load).
  • No permission required from building plan sanctioning authority for rooftop solar power plants.

Installation Checklist

How to Get Solar Installed

  • Is your site suitable for solar energy? Check for shading and roof orientation.
