Who We Are

Viniva Solar: Your Partner in Clean Energy

Viniva Solar is a trusted name in solar energy solutions, based in Kothamangalam, Kerala. We are committed to providing premium solar products that meet international quality standards. Serving residential, commercial, and industrial sectors, we make clean, renewable energy accessible and affordable for all.

For premium solar solutions customized for you, reach out to us today.

Our journey began in 2015 when we partnered with Havells India Limited to install an 80 kV on-grid solar system at Bharat Mata College, Kerala. This landmark project set the foundation for our impactful work in renewable energy.

We are honoured to collaborate with renowned brands, including BMW, Volkswagen, MG, Nissan, Grand Cinemas, EVM Group, and Jos Alukkas Jewelers, delivering high-quality solar solutions tailored to their needs.
