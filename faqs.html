Why Go Solar?

Benefits of Solar Energy

  • Power of the sun can power your daily energy needs without interruptions.
  • Massive reduction in CO2 emissions. Create cleaner and greener energy.
  • Higher ROI and short payback period.
  • Reduce your electricity bills drastically.
  • Protects you from future rises in power costs.
  • Solar panels ensure it's always lights on—dependable and always available.
  • Increases the valuation of your property.

Kerala Solar Policy

State Policy Highlights

  • Maximum sanctioned load: 99%
  • Maximum load: 2 MW
  • Electricity generated by rooftop solar system shall not be more than 90% of the electricity consumption at the end of the settlement period.
  • Cumulative capacity of local distribution transformer: MAXIMUM 30%
  • Net Metering: ALLOWED
  • Solar rooftop mandatory for new residential buildings with plot size more than 500 sq. yards and for commercial offices, malls, complexes (3% to 5% of sanctioned load).
  • No permission required from building plan sanctioning authority for rooftop solar power plants.

Installation Checklist

How to Get Solar Installed

  • Is your site suitable for solar energy? Check for shading and roof orientation.
  • What is the age of the roof?
  • Start with an energy audit to improve efficiency.
  • Check utility policies for grid integration and net metering.
  • Ask your contractor for safety practices, licensing, and insurance documentation.
  • Review all costs, financing terms, warranties, and contact information.
